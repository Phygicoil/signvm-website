SIGNVM Pitch Deck
01 — The Problem

"Your mother gets a call.
It's your voice.
She can't tell it's not you."

AI can now clone a voice in three seconds. Replicate a face from a photograph. Forge a signature from a sample. Digital identity — passwords, biometrics, face scans — is becoming unreliable.

The question is no longer can someone fake you. It's how do you prove you're real?

02 — The Insight

You can't steal
possession remotely.

Information leaks. Objects don't.

For four thousand years, a signet ring pressed into wax was the most trusted form of identity. Not a password. Not a face scan. An object you hold.

Physical presence is the last unforgeable signal.

03 — The Solution

The modern signet.

A precision-machined brass antenna with an embedded cryptographic chip. No battery. No screen. No expiry. The grooves on the ring face are the antenna. The antenna is the circuit. Nothing is hidden.

CNC micro-milling the antenna trace. The ring is the technology.

04 — How It Works

Tap to sign.

Hold the ring to any NFC-enabled phone. The phone powers the chip. The chip generates a unique cryptographic signature — verifiable on-chain via LUKSO Universal Profile.

Proof you were there, at that moment, in that place. No app. No pairing. No charging. Just tap.

Tap the ring. The phone reads the cryptographic signature instantly.

05 — The Network

Trust scales through
constellations.

You have a ring. Your mother has a ring. Either of you can demand proof the other is real. One ring verifies you. A network of rings protects everyone you trust.

01
