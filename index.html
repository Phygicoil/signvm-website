How it works
01

Tap

Hold the ring to any NFC-enabled phone. The phone powers the chip. The chip generates a unique cryptographic signature. No battery. No app. No pairing.

02

Verify

The signature is written to the blockchain. Immutable. Timestamped. Tied to your Universal Profile. Proof you were there, at that moment, in that place.

03

Authorise

Grant access. Confirm identity. Approve an AI agent to act on your behalf. The ring is the only thing that can say yes.
