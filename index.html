A signet ring pressed into wax was the most trusted form of identity for four thousand years.

AI can fake your voice, your face, your handwriting. It cannot fake a ring on your hand.

Tap. Sign. Verify.

The ring signs things. That's it.

A verification challenge. A transaction. A mandate for an AI agent. An access token. A contract.

All the same cryptographic operation.

No battery. No app. No pairing. The grooves on the face are the antenna. The antenna is the circuit. It draws power from your phone at the moment of contact.
